Interactions & Animations

Scan frame: Corner brackets subtly pulse (opacity 0.7→1.0, 1.5s loop). When text is detected, brackets snap to the text region boundary with spring animation.
Live overlay: Translation cards fade in (200ms) with backdrop blur. Cards reposition smoothly when new text blocks are detected. Stale cards fade out after 3s.
Shutter: Quick flash overlay (white, 100ms fade) on capture. Results slide up from bottom with spring physics.
Mode toggle: Gradient pill slides between Photo/Live Scan with layout animation (200ms ease).
Context hints: Italic descriptions under translations fade in 200ms after the main translation appears. Powered by location context (menu items get food descriptions).
