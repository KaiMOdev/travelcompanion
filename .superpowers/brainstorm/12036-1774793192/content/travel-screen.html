Interactions & Animations

Mic button: Idle = gentle glow pulse (2s ease). Press-and-hold starts recording. Glow intensifies + radiates outward. Transforms to red stop button.
Waveform: 12 bars with gradient fill, heights driven by audio amplitude. Bars animate with spring physics (damping: 0.6, stiffness: 200).
Chat bubbles: Slide in from left (source) or right (translation) with spring animation. Fade + translateY(8px) → translateY(0). Duration: 300ms.
Language swap: Rotate arrow icon 180° + cross-fade language labels. Spring physics on the swap button rotation.
Replay audio: Tap translation bubble footer → brief ripple on bubble + audio playback. Cyan speaker icon pulses during playback.
Auto-scroll: New bubbles trigger smooth scroll to bottom. ScrollView with inverted list pattern for optimal performance.
