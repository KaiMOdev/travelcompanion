Interactions & Animations

Active speaker: Avatar gets a pulsing glow ring in their color. Mini waveform bars animate next to the speaker name. Glow fades when they stop speaking.
New utterance: Slides in from bottom with spring animation (translateY(16px) → 0, opacity 0→1, 250ms). Auto-scrolls to latest.
Timer: Green dot pulses slowly (opacity 0.6→1.0, 2s). Timer counts up in real-time next to it.
Session end: Subtitles fade, summary card slides up from bottom with session stats. Transcript compacts from full subtitle view to dense list.
Speaker colors: Assigned from a 6-color gradient palette: blue, violet, emerald, amber, rose, teal. Each speaker gets the next available color. Colors persist for the session.
